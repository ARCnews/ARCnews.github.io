Asean Rohingya Center

ARC is dedicated to advocate for the rights of the Rohingya people by providing a platform whereby forum and dialogue on Rohingya issues can be addressd such as pursuing outreach activities eg: organising seminars, conferences, colloquiums, workshops, networking events, publication and dissemination of information. In other words ARC will provide intellectual forum to nurture and promote Rohingya issues.
