Myanmar Permanent Peoples' Tribunal: The Verdict

Kuala Lumpur: September 22, 2017
President of the International Movement for a Just World (JUST) Dr. Chandra Muzaffar, closing statements from the chair of the Malaysian Organizing Committee for the Myanmar Permanent People’s Tribunal (PPT) Concluding Session held at the Universiti Malaya Legal Faculty moot court. The entire proceeding was held here from 18th-22nd September, 2017.
Whereby the Official Opening Session of the Permanent Peoples’ Tribunal on Myanmar's State Crimes against Rohingya, Kachin and other Groups was hosted at the School of Law, Queen Mary University of London, from 6th-7th March 2017.
